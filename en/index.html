Frequently asked questions

What businesses usually ask Goatify.

What exactly does Goatify do?

Goatify is a business technology company. We build CRM systems, AI agents, automated replies, WhatsApp and voice automation, websites, e-commerce, custom applications, internal portals, productivity tools, email marketing, social media systems, SEO and integrations.

Is Portal Goatify really free to try?

Yes. Portal Goatify is the free entry point to organize customers, projects, tasks and productivity. Advanced CRM, AI agents, integrations, automation or custom development can be added according to the business need.

Can Goatify connect WhatsApp, calls, forms and a CRM?

Yes. A project can connect WhatsApp, web chat, social channels, AI voice calls, audio notes, forms, calendars, email, CRM records, Google Sheets, APIs, payments and webhooks—depending on scope and provider availability.

Do you build custom websites and apps?

Yes. Goatify builds conversion-focused websites, e-commerce, installable PWAs, internal systems, dashboards, portals and custom web applications. We can also connect them to CRM, automation and marketing workflows.

Can you work with companies in the United States?

Yes. Goatify works remotely with businesses in the United States, Latin America and Spain, with communication available in English and Spanish.

How do we know which solution to start with?

Start with a free consultation. We review the business, customer journey and current tools, then recommend the smallest useful implementation—Portal, CRM, automation, website, app, marketing or a connected combination.
