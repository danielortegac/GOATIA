# GOATIA V19 — English global landing rebuild

## Why `/en/` exists
The `/en/` URL is a separate, crawlable English version intended for English-language searchers in the United States and other markets. It is paired with the Spanish home using `hreflang`.

## What was wrong before
The first English page was too short and leaned too heavily on CRM language. It did not represent the complete Goatify ecosystem.

## What changed
- Completely rebuilt `en/index.html`; it is no longer a thin CRM-like page.
- Added the main home video (`Main final.Mp4`) as the hero background.
- Added visual assets already used elsewhere in the website.
- Added full sections for CRM, AI agents, WhatsApp automation, voice calls, websites, apps, Portal Goatify, productivity, social media, advertising, email marketing, industries, portfolio, partners and the product ecosystem.
- Added product links for Portal Goatify, QLASE, goatiFIT, Flow, Goatify Meet and Goatify Mailing.
- Added international service coverage for Latin America, the United States and Spain.
- Reinforced that Portal Goatify can be tried free.
- Expanded visible FAQ content and FAQ structured data.
- Expanded Organization, WebPage, Service and ItemList structured data.
- Kept `Goatify` as the primary brand and `Goatify IA` / `Goatify AI` as alternate names.
- Kept canonical and reciprocal language alternates.
- Updated the `/en/` sitemap entry to weekly priority 0.95.

## Scope
The visual/content rebuild changes `en/index.html`. The sitemap entry for `/en/` was also updated. Existing pages and the V18 pricing contrast fix were preserved.
