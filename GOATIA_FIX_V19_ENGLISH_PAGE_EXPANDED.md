# GOATIA V19 — English page expanded

## Why `/en/` exists
The English URL is a separate crawlable language version of the Goatify overview. It was created because IP/browser-based translation may not expose every language version reliably to search crawlers. It is not meant to replace the Spanish website and it is not a duplicate of the CRM page.

## Changes made only to the English experience
- Rebuilt `en/index.html` from scratch as a broad Goatify overview.
- Added the same main video used on the Spanish home (`Main final.Mp4`).
- Added existing Goatify visual assets for CRM, automation, portals, websites, apps, strategy and portfolio.
- Added full sections for CRM, AI agents, automated responses, WhatsApp, voice calls, websites, apps, productivity, Portal Goatify, social media, mailing and industry solutions.
- Added the Goatify ecosystem products: Goatify App, GO, goatiFIT, Flow and Goatify Meet.
- Added United States, Latin America and Spain coverage plus country-level service chips.
- Added links to the existing service pages and a Spanish-language switch.
- Expanded Open Graph, Twitter metadata, AI summary fields, structured data, FAQ and VideoObject schema.
- Updated `llms-full.txt` so the English URL is described as the complete English overview.

## What was not changed
- No Spanish page content or layout was altered.
- The pricing contrast fix from V18 remains intact.
- The English page keeps its own canonical URL and reciprocal language references.
